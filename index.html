✦ Our Causes

Where your gift goes

Every donation is directed to one of our core pillars of change.

Clean Water

Delivering safe drinking water to remote villages through sustainable well-building and filtration programs.

Education

Building schools, training teachers, and providing scholarships so every child can access quality learning.

Food Security

Supporting local farmers and food banks to eliminate hunger and create long-term nutritional stability.

We believe in the dignity of every human being.

Founded in 2008, RiseTogether has grown from a small grassroots movement into a globally recognised charity. Our work is rooted in compassion, transparency, and measurable impact.

A mission born from purpose

Our founder, Sok Reakksaa, witnessed the devastating effects of extreme poverty during her work as a field physician. She returned home with one conviction: that systemic change required sustained, community-led effort — not charity tourism.

Today, RiseTogether partners with over 400 local organisations across 120 countries. We don't impose solutions; we amplify the voices of those who already know what their communities need.

Transparency Annual audited reports publicly available. 94% of every dollar reaches those in need.
Community-First Solutions designed with — never for — the communities we serve.
Long-Term Thinking We invest in infrastructure and education so change outlasts our presence.
